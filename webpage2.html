Effects

Effects of Unhealthy Practices

  1. Feeling of Stress, Anxiety and Depression

  2. Low Self - Esteem

  3. Difficulty Concentrating

  4. Mood Swings

  5. Sleep Disturbances

  6. Social Isolation

  7. Unhappiness & Decreased Enjoyment of Life and Quality of Life

Solutions

Solutions For Eradication
  • Mindfulness and Meditation
  • Daily Journaling and Reflective Practices
  • Developing Restorative Sleep routine
  • Balancing Diet(Eating Nutritious Food) and Drinking more Water
  • Reducing Screen time
  • Spend Time in Nature
  • Connect with Family and Friends
  • Spend some time for your Hobbies
